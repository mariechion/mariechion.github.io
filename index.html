I am a Research Associate in biostatistics at the University of Cambridge, in the MRC Biostatistics Unit, in William Astle's group, and a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Trinity Hall College.

I enjoy working on interdisciplinary projects. My main research interests are:

  • High-dimensional data
  • Missing values & imputation methods
  • (Prote)omics data: differential analysis, uncertainty quantification
  • Genetic epidemiology: GxE interactions, dimension reduction
  • Haematology and transfusion medicine applications (Haem-Match)

And I am always eager to discover and work on other methods and applications!
